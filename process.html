Light Hydrocarbon Extraction


There are various methods of extraction that exist, but using light hydrocarbons is the number one choice for Tsunami. It is the most effecient way to produce the quantity and quality of extracts we desire. This is due to the polarity of the hydrocarbons being used and the molecules that exist within the cannabis plant. This process allows our extracts to truly capture the valuable traits of the material such as cannabinoids (THC, CBD, etc) and terpenes (Limonene, Myrcene, etc).
