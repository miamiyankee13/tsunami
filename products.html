Live Resin

Manufacturing live resin extracts allows us to always capture the most accurate flavor and smell profile of the material being processed. The term live resin refers to the plant still living when it is extracted. While the material may not technically still be alive, as soon as it is harvested it is frozen and remains frozen throughout the extraction process. This allows us to preserve valuable traits of the material such as the amount of trichomes, terpene profile, and aroma. These traits all carry over into the final product, creating a high-potent flavorful extract.

Sauce


Often referred to as HTFSE, or high terpene full spectrum extract, our sauce is the highest quality product we have to offer. Throughout the sauce making process, THCA crystals and terpenes naturally separate, forming two different products. These two products are mixed back together using a special ratio to form the perfect blend of crystals and terpenes.




Sugar


Often referred to as HCFSE, or high cannabinoid full spectrum extract, our sugar is made using almost the same process for our sauce. A few tweaks here and there - boom, we have ourselves some sugar. This product has a higher crystal to terpene ratio than our sauce. If you find sauce to be too flavorful for your liking, then sugar should be right up your alley!

Badder


Call us old school, but you can't go wrong with some good ol' fashioned badder. Badder is basically shatter taken to the next level. With a little heat and agitation we can turn a beautiful, glassy slab of shatter into a stable, terpy slab of badder. While it may not be as sexy as our sauce or sugar, it is definitley a wave worth catching.




HTE Vape Cartridges


Due to our special ratios used during our sauce/sugar processes, we will sometimes end up with an excess amount of terpenes. These terpenes are then used to fill our vape cartridges. Vaping this oil tastes like you are dabbing right out of the cartridge!
